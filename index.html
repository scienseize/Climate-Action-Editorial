13 SDG Climate Action

UN Sustainable Development Goal

SDG 13: Take Urgent Action to Combat Climate Change

Adopted in 2015 as part of the 2030 Agenda, SDG 13 calls on all countries to take immediate action to combat climate change and its devastating impacts. It recognises that climate change is a defining issue of our time.

Target 13.1

Strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-related hazards and natural disasters.

Target 13.2

Integrate climate change measures into national policies, strategies and planning.

Target 13.3

Improve education, awareness-raising and capacity on climate change mitigation and adaptation.

Target 13.a

Mobilise $100 billion/yr to address the needs of developing countries in climate action.
